## I16-1. Introduction of Geometry to Target Fields

### Background

MathQForm currently covers 4 fields: DE (differential equations), PS (probability and statistics), RA (real analysis), and AA (abstract algebra).
Adding geometry would provide comprehensive coverage of the major areas of mathematics.
With GT (geometry and topology), the project would cover 5 fields, each representing a distinct mathematical domain.

### Requirements

- Analyze the impact of adding geometry to the current 4-field structure
- If appropriate, update schema and planning documents
  - With 5 fields: 100 Items each (500 Items total)

#### Deliverable Format

Document / Schema

#### Related Issues

I16-2 (Reassessment of mathematical field labeling)

### Completion Criteria

- [x] Analyze the current field structure and impact of adding geometry
- [x] Update schema for 5-field support
- [x] Revise planning documents for 5-field structure

---

### Response

Introduced GT (geometry_topology) as the 5th field.

**Field structure**: DE, PS, RA, AA, GT — 5 fields (100 Items each, 500 total)
- Budget-neutral: 125×4 → 100×5 = 500 Items (no change in total)
- B4–M1 level: point-set topology, differential geometry of curves/surfaces, introductory algebraic topology

**Updated files**:
- 6 schema files: added GT to field enums and ID patterns
- 9 planning documents: updated field lists, Item allocation, and budget breakdowns to 5-field structure
